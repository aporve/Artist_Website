About Anupama

Art to me is a way of life... and my way of expression.

Hailing from a culturally inclined Bengali family, along with academics, art in its various forms of expression has always been an integral part of growing up. Keeping in mind that 'success' used to be considered more of a certainty if one's academic side was strong, I pursued Economics till I got my Masters degree.

But painting was a part of me all through. Now I am more focussed in pursuing this and am enjoying each day as I get to follow my passion. I love to experiment with my expression and cater to Commission Art.

In due course of time I have developed my style of Abstract Expressionism through drip art technique... which is colorful, vibrant and comes with a unique subtle message. Painting and art has never failed to give me direction. 

My art is unique... not influenced by what is 'available' out there. I draw as I feel... depending on my mood and emotions.

Contact me at anupama@anupama-art.com for sales and exhibition enquiries.
